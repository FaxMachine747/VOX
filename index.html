// Game Nights

How Game Nights Work

📅
Sign Up

Events are posted in Discord. Click Interested on the event to sign up. No invite needed — just show up.

🏆
Win & Earn

Win a game night and earn 20 VOX Coins per player in the lobby. A 20-player lobby pays out 400 coins to the winner.

⚔️
Tournaments

Free to enter. Optionally stake VOX Coins — win and pocket 10× your entry. High risk, high reward.

🎲
Gambling

Bet your coins in gambling games. Max 5 bets per day, cap of 100 coins per bet. Use the 🪙vox-coins channel.
